Korean has terminations which are basically 'a','aseo','aya','ado'. Like this, Korean has terminations 'eo','eosea','eoyeo','eodo'. Many people use a termination 'seo',but Korean doesn't have a termination '-seo', but it has '-eoseo' instead of '-seo'. So, we should write 'doeeoseo' less 'dwea', not 'doeseo'.

As above, 'eo' of 'eoseo' abbreviates and omits oftenly. That's why many Korean people confuse to use 'dwea' and 'doeseo' wiht not knowing Korean termintaions. But, acutually, that is a really simple anwser.
